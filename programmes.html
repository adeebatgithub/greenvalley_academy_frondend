VOCATIONAL TRAINING

Diploma in Computer Applications

Our Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA) is a career-oriented programme offering fundamental knowledge and practical expertise in modern computing, designed to make students industry-ready.

  • Computer Fundamentals
  • Microsoft PowerPoint
  • Internet Technologies
  • Graphic Design Basics
  • Hardware & Networking
  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Microsoft Word
  • Microsoft Excel

ELECTRICAL TECHNICIAN

The Electrical Technician certification equips students with hands-on skills in electrical wiring, maintenance, installation, and troubleshooting, paving the way for a dynamic career in technical industries.

  • Electrical Wiring Fundamentals
  • Safety & Hazard Prevention
  • Circuit Troubleshooting
  • Equipment Maintenance
  • Appliance Repair
  • Industrial Wiring Basics
  • Power Distribution
  • Testing & Quality Checks

DIPLOMA IN GRAPHIC DESIGNING

The Diploma in Graphic Designing nurtures creative talent by teaching industry-standard design software, visual communication principles, and digital artistry to prepare students for the creative sector.

  • Visual Communication
  • Typography & Color Theory
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Layout Design
  • UI/UX Basics
  • Brand Identity Design
  • Portfolio Development

DIPLOMA IN FITNESS TRAINER

Our Diploma in Fitness Training provides comprehensive knowledge in human anatomy, exercise physiology, and nutrition, empowering students to become certified, professional fitness instructors.

  • Human Anatomy & Physiology
  • Exercise Kinesiology
  • Diet & Sports Nutrition
  • Strength & Conditioning
  • Injury Prevention
  • Personal Training Strategies
  • Group Fitness Leadership
  • Client Assessment

DIPLOMA IN CYBERSECURITY

The Diploma in Cybersecurity is a specialized, job-oriented programme designed to equip students with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills required to defend networks, systems, and data against emerging cyber threats.

  • Network Security Fundamentals
  • Ethical Hacking Basics
  • Cryptography
  • Risk Management
  • Cyber Laws & Ethics
  • Incident Response
  • Malware Analysis
  • Cloud Security

SOFT SKILLS

Beyond academic excellence, we prioritize the holistic development of our students. Our comprehensive soft skills programmes are designed to build physical vitality, enhance communication, and foster leadership, ensuring our graduates are well-rounded and ready for real-world challenges.
